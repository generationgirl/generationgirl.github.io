Who we are

We’re a non-profit organization helping to shape and empower future of women tech trendsetters. We work with generous sponsors that has been helping us making this all happen!

Generation Girl was founded by women leaders of the Indonesian tech world. We know it’s hard being in a dominated field, so we want to make the journey easier for the next generation and see more female trailblazers!

We want to introduce Computer Science and Engineering to girls at a young age and show them that it’s the cool, next big thing!

On my first day of work in an Indonesian tech company, no one believed I was actually a developer. My new (almost all male) colleagues thought I was someone's girlfriend or little sister coming to visit their office, but no one assumed that I was their new teammate. Throughout my career, people have thought that my successes come from "batting my eyelashes" or just the simple fact that I'm a girl. My ultimate goal is to have so many women in STEM fields that we start saying 'tech is such a girls club' ;)

Nadine Siregar

Being a software engineer is probably one of the most friendliest careers for a woman due to its work from home policy across any companies out there in general. I was stunned by the ratio of women in tech here in Indonesia compared to men, and I want to make it a goal to empower girls by teaching them to code from early on. I did not have this sort of a summer program when I was young but if I did, I would totally sign up for it!

Vania Alfitri
