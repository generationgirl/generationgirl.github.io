Winter Club Topics

    

Build A Website (Intermediate)

16 - 20 December • Week 1

Ever wondered what it takes to build a website? Join us to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript further. This topic is suitable for those who already have the basic skills in creating a website! Master the art of website development now by joining this class!

Web & App Design (Beginner & Intermediate)

16 - 20 December • Week 1

23 - 28 December • Week 2

This one is for all the designers out there! A user’s experience on a site or app is just as important as the features on it. This is a non-technical topic (very little coding involved) where you will learn how to create beautiful web and mobile app designs, interactive flows, and prototypes using Figma.

Intro to Animation (Beginner)

23 - 28 December • Week 2

A new topic has arrived just for you! Presenting… Intro to Animation! Calling all who’s passionate about art, this class is specially made just for you! You’re going to learn more about basics of modeling using Blender app. We’re going to introduce you to basics of animation, logo animation, compositing and also rendering! Sounds exciting huh? Sign up now!

Mentors


Calling all female tech leaders!

Whether you’re a student or a young professional hoping to pass on your knowledge, we want YOU to inspire the next generation of techies! Build a week-long curriculum that you’ll teach to a new batch of Rookies! Choose how many weeks you want to teach that same curriculum. Don’t worry, you don’t have to teach all six weeks! Choose from the topics above and the language (English or Bahasa Indonesia) you’re most comfortable in. Get involved and apply now!

Mentor Applications are due by December 6th, 2019




Rookies


Calling all 9th -11th grade girls!

Not sure what you’re doing this holiday? If you’re a 9th to 12th grade girl, join our Winter Club to learn a new skill! All the topics above are taught in both English and Bahasa Indonesia. We’ve also got some surprise activities and field trips for you, too. Everything is completely FREE (including lunch and field trips!), and you don’t need any technical experience to join! You’ll leave the Club feeling fresh and empowered. Who wouldn’t want that?

Rookie Registration ends December 11th, 2019
