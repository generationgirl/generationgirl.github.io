About Us


Generation Girl is a non-profit organization aimed at introducing young girls to the STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) fields through fun, educational Holiday Clubs. Our goal is to shape future female leaders & empower these girls to make their mark in male-dominated fields! We are based in Jakarta, Indonesia, but stay tuned for future locations!

Our Clubs

    


Winter Club 2019

16 - 28 December 2019

It’s time to spend your holiday with Gen G!

Winter Club 2019 is here to give you our warmest hugs this December! This time, we’re giving out a new topic for you to learn - Intro to Animation! For those of you who have already learned Web Development and Web & App Design, we’re also offering new intermediate classes! If you’re a 9th - 12th grader female, you can absolutely join our club! Mentors are also welcome to join us this Winter to teach our kids some cool stuff, so if you’re currently in uni or working in IT field, you’re welcome to sign up!

Summer Club 2019

17 June - 26 July 2019

Keep cool with us this holiday at our FREE Summer Club!

From June 17 to July 26 2019, we will be opening our doors for another batch of 9th to 11th grade Rookies! Our Mentors will be building and teaching curriculums for a variety of topics that you can choose from. Although the Club runs for six weeks, you can pick and choose which weeks you want to join and which topics to learn each week. Pssst, we’re looking for university students and fresh graduates to become our Mentors 😉

Winter Club 2018

17 - 21 December 2018

‘Tis the season to learn something new!

Get cozy with us this winter holiday and learn web development in this one-week Club! We’ll also be hosting a self-defense class along with some other surprises too. All girls aged 12-16 are welcome to join as a Rookie and anyone with web development experience can apply as a Mentor. Come one, come all!
